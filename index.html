Example Dashboards Pro's & Cons

Pros

  • Important information displayed on big dials for easy reading (MPH, RPM, Fuel, ...)
  • Lots of signals to indicate various states (Check Engine, Check Oil, ...)

Cons

  • Too much information at once
  • Lots of signals that arent easy to understand

Features & Interactions

Common Features

Features found on most cars:

  • Speed (MPH)
  • Speed (RPM)
  • Fuel Level
  • Mile to the Galon
  • Miles Traveled
  • Seatbelt Warning

Rare Features

Features found on very few cars:

  • Current Gear
  • Tire Pressure

User Interactions

Controls that users often interact with:

  • Change Item (Cars that have digital displays, can cycle through MPG, and Air Pressure, and so on.)
  • Dash Lights (Cars using analog displays that need to be lit at night.)

Mistakes in Current Designs

Some common mistakes found in current designs:

  • Too much information at once
  • Hard to understand signal lights
  • Hard to read dials and interfaces

Low Fidelity Design

For my design the goal was to create a basic dashboard giving the users the most information while not burdening them with information they dont need at the time.
